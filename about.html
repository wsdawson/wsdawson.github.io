About me


I'm Will, a digital creative operating across UX and UI design disciplines.



As a designer I love what I do and thrive on challenges, whether its crafting eye-catching digital ad campaigns, or developing solutions for complex projects such as online learning platforms, intranet systems and e-commerce.

I have worked broadly across digital, with competencies in creative concepting, information architecture, wireframing, UI design and front-end development. I enjoy working collaboratively with clients and project teams to create enjoyable and successful digital experiences, and have delivered successful digital campaigns for a range of clients including Lloyds Banking Group, BP, and Computershare.

Experience

In my updated role of UX Designer at Six my position has expanded to oversee the entire lifecycle of digital projects from the strategic and conceptual stages through to solution and delivery. I collaborate with a diverse team of developers, copywriters, product managers, and strategists to craft innovative and creative solutions for clients such as Lloyds Banking Group, BP, Castrol and Computershare.

In my role as Digital Creative Designer I scoped, concepted and crafted design solutions for a range of digital products. I produced information architectures, wireframes, UI design and front-end development solutions for websites; scamped and designed banners and interstitial advertising for clients such as Lloyds Bank, and worked effectively with brand guidelines to produce innovative digital solutions for a broad range of clients including Computershare, University of West England, Sunlife and Schwartz.
