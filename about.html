Work About

Hi, I'm Will. I am a designer who loves crafting innovative and engaging digital experiences.

I am a senior digital creative operating across UX and UI disciplines. I have several years experience in agency and client-side roles, and I enjoy working collaboratively with clients and cross-functional project teams to create engaging, enjoyable and inclusive digital products.

Experience

I have worked broadly across digital, crafting digital products, websites, apps and ad campaigns for both 'big ticket' clients and SMEs. My involvement in projects has ranged from creative ideation and UX discovery, through to prototyping, UI design, and front-end development.

I currently work as a Senior UX/UI designer at Unite Students, defining their digital products experience, features and brand UI. My previous work includes creating game-ified learning platforms for Lloyds Bank and Castrol, a carbon calculator app for BP, and comprehensive UX/UI campaign work for UWE, Sunlife and Schwartz.

Approach

Rather than working in silo on the 'UX bit' or 'UI bit' of a project, I thrive on open, collaborative environments where project aims and end-goals can be defined, developed and deployed effectively by a collective team. From a UX perspective, I enjoy maintaining oversight of projects and working iteratively to continually improve on product experience and functionality. I believe in considered, research-orientated design, and take a human-centric approach to projects where purpose, accessibility, usability, and enjoyment are championed at each stage of the process.
