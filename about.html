About

Hi, I'm Will. I am a designer with a passion for crafting innovative and engaging digital experiences.

I am a digital creative operating across UX and UI disciplines. I have several years experience in agency and client-side, and I enjoy working collaboratively with clients and project teams to create engaging, enjoyable and inclusive digital products.

Experience

I have worked broadly across digital, crafting digital products, websites and campaigns for both 'big ticket' clients and SMEs. My involvement in projects has ranged from creative ideation and UX discovery, through to prototyping, UI design, and front-end development.

I have recently been working as a UX/UI designer on the Lloyds Bank Academy, a learning platform aimed at teaching users basic digital skills. My previous work includes web design and digital brand creation for investment platform Willis Owen, a carbon calculator app for BP, and comprehensive digital design work for MFL Direct's acquisition and customer retention campaigns.

Approach

Rather than working in silo on the 'UX bit' or 'UI bit' of a project, I thrive on open, collaborative environments where project aims and end-goals can be discussed, developed and deployed effectively by a collective team. From a UX perspective, I enjoy maintaining oversight of projects to ensure everything from layouts to interactive flourishes align with the defined purpose of the product and goals of the user. I believe in considered, research-orientated design, and take a human-centric approach to projects where purpose, accessibility, usability, and enjoyment are championed at each stage of the process.
